01 / About

I grew up in Northern Virginia in a family of six before leaving to obtain my BS in Engineering Mechanics at the University of Illinois.

For the last five years I've worked at SpaceX, starting as a structures engineer supporting the Dragon 2 spacecraft, and most recently managing a team developing structures, propulsion systems, and mechanisms for the Starship launch vehicle.

I am deeply interested in engineering problem solving, and the idea that I can help build a more exciting future with my skills. I also enjoy various athletic pursuits, tinkering, and of course my friends, family, and amazing partner Liz. Check out our shared travel blog here.

I am making this page in part as an engineering portfolio to reflect on some of my proudest accomplishments, but also as a home base for my self reflection. It's not necessarily made for anyone else to read, but I suppose I've linked to it from our blog, so hello if you're here :)
